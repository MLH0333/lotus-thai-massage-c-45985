Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book in advance?
Walk-ins are always welcome at Lotus Thai Massage, though booking in advance is recommended during busy periods to secure your preferred time and therapist.
What should I wear for a Thai massage?
For traditional Thai massage, you will be provided with comfortable, loose clothing to change into. For oil and balm treatments, you will be covered with towels throughout your session.
Can I choose my therapist?
You are welcome to request a specific therapist based on your preference, availability, and gender. Just let us know when you arrive or when you book.
How long are the sessions?
Sessions are available in 60, 90, and 120 minutes, depending on the treatment you choose.
Do you offer treatments for first-time massage guests?
Lotus Thai Massage is a great place for first-time guests. Our therapists are patient, attentive, and will guide you through the experience at your own pace.
Is there parking nearby?
Street parking is available on and around Wualai Road. Please note that Wualai Road is closed to traffic on Saturday evenings during the Night Market, so we recommend using a ride app like Grab or Bolt on those nights.
Does any of the staff speak English?
Our owner speaks fluent English, so you can comfortably communicate exactly what you need, how you like your massage, and any areas you would like your therapist to focus on.
